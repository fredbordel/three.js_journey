Lion

The lion head in the Cirque du Soleil show Echo is a masterpiece of craftsmanship. It’s made from a lightweight alloy, sculpted with precision to capture the majestic features of a lion. The mane is made from golden threads, each individually hand-sewn to create a shimmering spectacle under the stage lights.

Chèvre

The goat head, on the other hand, is a marvel of innovation. It’s crafted from biodegradable materials, symbolizing the harmony between nature and art. The horns are made from reclaimed wood, polished to a fine sheen, while the beard is made from organic cotton, dyed with natural pigments to achieve a realistic look.
